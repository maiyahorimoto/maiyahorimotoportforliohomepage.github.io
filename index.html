Art Styles

Drawing

The use of lines and markings, usually on a surface such as paper. Common tools in drawing are pencil, pen, charcoal, or pastel.

Painting

Applying color to a surface, such as canvas or paper, to express ideas or emotions. There are different mediums like acrylic, oil, or watercolor.

Ceramics

The art of shaping clay and then hardening it through firing in a kiln. Different techniques include hand-building and wheel throwing.
